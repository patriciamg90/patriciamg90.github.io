Service

Professional, institutional, and community engagement

I have been involved in outreach and service activities since my Ph.D. years, including leadership roles in student chapters (SIAM and AWM), conference and event organization, and institutional service. My service interests include building supportive communities for students in mathematics and data science, and strengthening inclusive pathways into STEM.
